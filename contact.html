Farah Shamout is an Assistant Professor Emerging Scholar at NYU Abu Dhabi. Shamout received her Bachelor of Science cum laude in Computer Engineering from NYU Abu Dhabi. She completed a DPhil in Engineering Science at Oxford as a Rhodes Scholar.

The Clinical AI Lab works on improving health outcomes by designing and developing smart diagnostic and prognostic systems. Our research interests cut across multiple disciplines, namely health, engineering, and computing. More about our research agenda.
