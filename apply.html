PhD positions


We hire PhD candidates via the competitive NYU Global PhD Fellowship for either the (i) PhD in Computer Science or (ii) PhD in Biomedical Engineering. The Global PhD Student Fellowship is in collaboration with the NYU Tandon School of Engineering. Students spend the first year in New York for courses, followed by three years of research in Abu Dhabi. The fellowship offers a competitive compensation package, covering tuition costs, housing in Abu Dhabi, a monthly stipend, as well as additional support for relocation.

Successful candidates are expected to have (i) a Master’s degree in computer science, engineering, or related fields, (ii) track-record of excellent academic achievement (GPA > 3.7), (iii) and an unwavering passion for transforming healthcare. Previous research experience is preferred.

If you are interested or have any questions, please complete this form.

Visiting graduate students


We occasionally hire graduate (MSc and PhD) students as visiting scholars. If you are interested, please share your CV, cover letter, and transcripts via email.

Full-time research positions


We are always searching for talented individuals to join us as post-doctoral associates, research assistants/associates, or research engineers. It's never too early to get in touch!

Post-doctoral Associate

Postdoctoral associates are expected to have (i) strong machine learning skills to improve model accuracy, robustness, and explainability, and (ii) exemplary passion to pursue research at the intersection of computing and healthcare. Apply directly on Interfolio.


Research Engineer

Research Engineers are expected to have (i) strong engineering skills to find innovative and cost-effective means to improve our research, and (ii) exemplary passion and motivation to pursue multidisciplinary research at the intersection of computing and healthcare. Apply directly on Interfolio.


Research Assistant/Associate

Research assistants are expected to have (i) hands-on experience with machine learning and data science via course projects, internships, or work experience, and (ii) exemplary passion and motivation to pursue multidisciplinary research at the intersection of computing and healthcare. Students who currently have or are in the process of completing a Bachelor’s or a Master’s degree in Computer Science, Mathematics, Computer Engineering, or relevant technical fields are eligible to apply, and the final title is determined based on years of experience. Apply directly on Interfolio.
