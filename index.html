About us

The Clinical AI Lab is based at the NYU Abu Dhabi campus. Led by Dr. Farah Shamout, our long-term goal is to improve health outcomes by designing and developing smart diagnostic and prognostic systems. Our research interests cut across multiple disciplines, namely health, engineering, and computing.

Our research agenda consists of two interrelated tracks:

  • conceptual machine learning frameworks to address current limitations of existing approaches

  • key thematic application areas mainly related to clinical decision support systems, cancer, and women's health.
