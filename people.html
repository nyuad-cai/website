Principal investigator



Farah Shamout

Farah Shamout is an Assistant Professor Emerging Scholar at NYU Abu Dhabi. Her current research interests include developing machine learning models to inform decision-making in healthcare, representation learning for large-scale multi-modal data, interpretability, and fairness and ethics of AI. Shamout completed her DPhil (Oxon PhD) in Engineering Science at the University of Oxford as a Rhodes Scholar at Balliol College. Her doctoral research focused on developing early warning models to predict in-hospital clinical deterioration, co-supervised by Prof. David Clifton and Dr. Tingting Zhu. Her examiners were Prof. Jens Rittscher and Prof. Richard Dobson. She also received her Bachelor of Science cum laude in Computer Engineering from New York University Abu Dhabi.
